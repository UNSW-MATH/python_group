           17th February, 1958.



The Editor,
Warrnambool Standard, 
WARRNAMBOOL.

Dear Sir,

     Will you please insert in the 
last issue prior to the 23rd February of 
the Warrnambool Standard the inclosed 
6-inch double column advertisement.

     Also enclosed is an article on the 
Opening of Parliament, which I hope you will 
find of interest to your readers.

        Yours faithfully,





          (J.M. Fraser)
